Bot Not Responding

Symptom: Your bot is online but doesn't respond to messages in the Telegram group or chat.

Checklist

Common Solutions

Sometimes, simply stopping and restarting the bot can resolve connectivity issues:

  1. Go to your Dashboard
  2. Find the bot and click "Stop"
  3. Wait 30 seconds
  4. Click "Start" to restart the bot

The way you specify the target group matters:

  • For public groups, use the format @groupname (with the @ symbol)
  • For private groups, use the exact group name
  • For direct chats, use the username (without @) or phone number
  • You can also use chat IDs for any type (e.g., -1001234567890)

Edit your bot settings to ensure you're using the correct format.

Your Telegram session might have expired or been terminated:

  1. Go to your Profile page
  2. Under "Telegram Verification", click "Re-verify"
  3. Follow the steps to verify your account again
  4. Try starting your bot again
Warning: If you're using multiple devices with Telegram, logging in elsewhere might terminate your current bot session.
